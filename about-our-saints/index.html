Meet our Managing Director📌 Official Announcement – The Launch of Saint Vision Group LLC

Revolutionizing Lending, Private Equity & Investment Solutions

Dear Business Owners, Investors, and Visionaries,


After two decades of experience in elite financial institutions, I am proud to announce the official launch of Saint Vision Group LLC—a firm built to redefine business lending, private equity, and high-yield investment solutions.


For years, I’ve had the privilege of working with some of the most respected financial institutions, including JP Morgan Chase, Oppenheimer & Co., Wells Fargo Private Bank, and leading my own ventures such as Asher Real Estate & Development. Throughout my career, I have managed portfolios, structured complex financial strategies, and helped business owners and investors maximize their wealth. However, I recognized a major gap in the industry—the need for a firm that provides capital solutions without limits, investment opportunities that truly perform, and financial expertise that clients can trust.


That is why Saint Vision Group LLC was born.


💡 Saint Vision Group LLC is more than just a financial firm—it’s a movement, a strategic force dedicated to delivering the financial tools and resources entrepreneurs and investors need to scale, succeed, and secure generational wealth.


🚀 Why Saint Vision Group LLC?

✔ Lending Without Limits – Business loans, real estate financing, and private capital structured to meet the unique needs of every client. Whether it’s a small business expansion loan or a multi-million-dollar commercial real estate deal, we get it done.


✔ High-Yield Investment Strategies – Offering secure 9-12% fixed returns through expertly structured investment vehicles backed by due diligence and institutional-grade financial strategies.


✔ A Nationwide Presence & Expansion – With fully operational offices in Huntington Beach & Newport Beach, we are now expanding into Phoenix & Flagstaff, positioning ourselves as a premier nationwide financial powerhouse.


✔ Industry-Leading Expertise – With over 20 years of experience working in private banking, real estate development, investment strategy, and structured finance, our team leverages cutting-edge financial solutions to structure deals that other firms can’t.


✔ Tailored Wealth & Capital Strategies – Our private equity and investment divisions ensure that both businesses and investors benefit from custom-tailored financial solutions designed to create long-term financial success.


🌎 A Community-Centered Approach

✔ Empowering Communities Through Financial Growth – We believe in the power of financial success to uplift individuals, businesses, and communities. Every project we support is a step toward economic development, job creation, and sustainable wealth-building.


✔ Investment in Projects That Matter – Saint Vision Group LLC is committed to funding initiatives that enhance the quality of life for individuals and families, from real estate developments that strengthen neighborhoods to business expansions that create lasting economic impact.


✔ Unwavering Transparency & Integrity – Trust is the foundation of everything we do. We uphold the highest ethical standards, ensuring that our clients, partners, and investors have full confidence in our strategies, recommendations, and financial solutions.
